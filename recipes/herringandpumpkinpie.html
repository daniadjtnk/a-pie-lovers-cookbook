Herring And Pumpkin Pie

WHY YOU SHOULD COOK THIS RECIPE

The Herring and Pumpkin Pie is an intriguing dish that fuses the rich flavors of smoked herring with the earthy sweetness of pumpkin, creating a unique and savory pie experience.

INGREDIENT

  • Pie Crust
    • 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
    • ½ teaspoon of salt
    • ½ cup of unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
    • 4-6 tablespoons of ice-cold water
  • Filling:
    • 1 cup of smoked herring, deboned and flaked
    • 2 cups of diced pumpkin
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • ½ cup of heavy cream
    • 2 eggs
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • Olive for garnish (optional)

HOW TO MAKE EARL GREY PIE

  1. Prepare the Crust:
    • In a bowl, mix the flour and salt. Cut in the chilled butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Gradually add water, mixing until the dough forms. Shape it into a disk, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the Filling:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Roll out the chilled dough and line a pie dish. Prick the base with a fork and blind bake for 15 minutes.
    • In a pan, sauté the onion and garlic until soft. Add the diced pumpkin and cook until tender. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
    • In a bowl, whisk together eggs, heavy cream, salt, and pepper. Fold in the flaked herring and the cooked pumpkin mixture. Pour the filling into the partially baked crust.
  3. Bake the Pie:
    • Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the filling is set and the crust is golden brown. Allow the pie to cool for a few minutes before serving.
  4. Serve:
    • Garnish with olive if desired. Serve slices of the Herring and Pumpkin Pie warm, allowing its intriguing blend of flavors to captivate your taste buds.

The Herring and Pumpkin Pie introduces a delightful interplay of smoky, savory herring with the natural sweetness of pumpkin, creating a pie that stands out as a savory masterpiece in the world of culinary exploration.
